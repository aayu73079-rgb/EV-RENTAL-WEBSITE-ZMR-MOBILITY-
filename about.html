Key Milestones & Future Focus


Current Focus: The company's focus for the year 2024 is to achieve a fleet of 1,200 vehicles.
Future Vision: The presentation indicates a future focus on profitability, and expanding operations to cities like Lucknow and Jaipur to meet demand.
Technology Integration: A key achievement is the use of AI and IoT-enabled live vehicle tracking to manage the fleet.
Business Model: The company operates on an "Asset Lite" model and focuses on vehicle sales through DCO, LTO, and RTO models.

Expansion Plan :
Year 2025: Expand to 4 new cities, with a target of 6,000 vehicles.
Year 2026: Expand to Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities, with a target of 15,000 vehicles.
